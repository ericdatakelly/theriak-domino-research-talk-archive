## EricKelly

To subscribe to a single topic within a forum:

- Click on the topic title to go to the topic
- Look for the "tool/wrench" dropdown menu just below the topic title (or at the bottom of the page)
- Click the menu and select the checkbox "Subscribe topic" (it will change to an empty checkbox with "Unsubscribe topic" after you are subscribed)
