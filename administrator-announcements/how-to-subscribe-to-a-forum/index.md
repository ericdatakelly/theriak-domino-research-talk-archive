If you would like to receive notifications when any post or reply is published in a forum, click "Subscribe forum" at the bottom of the forum page (note that the checkbox will be empty after you subscribe):

![subscribe_forum.png]({{ '/assets/attachments/9_subscribe_forum.png' | relative_url }})
