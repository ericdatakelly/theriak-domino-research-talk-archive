Hi Renato,

There is one in the Github databases site of Doug Tinkham, it is in the td-ds633-ef21-v03 file. It is the Diener et al. 2007 model, but it has no Fe3+.
I used it for Crd-Oam rocks, and I got similar results using this as with Perple_X and TC.
